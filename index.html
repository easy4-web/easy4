Welcome to Easy4

Easy4 Disc Golf Club calls Järta Discgolfipark in Märjamaa home — one of Estonia's finest disc golf experiences. Set amid the natural beauty of the Estonian countryside, our course challenges and rewards players of every skill level.

Whether you're a seasoned ace hunter or throwing a disc for the very first time, our community welcomes you. Come join us on the fairways.

The Course

Järta Discgolfipark, Märjamaa — a scenic layout through authentic Estonian nature.
